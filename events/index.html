RULES

  1. A group must comprise of minimum 8 participants and maximum 15 participants.
  2. Dance for western / fusion music can be performed under this category.

  4. Duration for Prelims: Minimum of 6 minutes and Maximum of 8 minutes is allowed.
  5. Duration for Finals: Minimum of 10 minutes and Maximum of 12 minutes is allowed.
  6. The teams selected for finals must present a different performance. The prelims sequence cannot be repeated.
  7. Song tracks to be submitted in a pendrive (strictly in MP3 format) in ready to play condition at least 1 hour before the event begins.
  8. Creativity in dance sequences and music will be given due weightage.
  9. Stage cleanliness and discipline should be maintained.
  10. The decision of the organizing committee is final and participants must abide by it.
